How to append resources to the éToile Curriculum (for researchers and students)



  1. Login on éToile. If you don’t have a login/password, Register first, then Login.

  2. Go to the “Add new resource” page (option “Share Resources”, on the top left menu).

  3. Fill the form for submitting a new resource, including a Title, a web Link, and associate this resource to a Challenge in the éToile Curriculum.

  4. You can submit more resources.

  5. Go to the éToile Curriculum (left menu) and check if the resources are well appended in their corresponding challenges.

  6. Appending new resources to the éToile Curriculum has increased your reputation. Go to the Reputation page (return to the homepage, and then choose “Reputation of users” in the top left menu) for checking your reputation level.
