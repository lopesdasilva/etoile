How to Sign Up to an éToile Module


  1. Login to éToile. If you don’t have a login/password, Register first, then Login.

  2. Go to Modules (option “Modules” on the top menu bar).

  3. Open the page of the module you want to sign up.

  4. Click on “Sign Up” (blue button on top-right of the page).

  5. The module will appear on the left menu “My Modules”. You can now open the module page and participate in all of its activities, such as aced to the lessons, news and resources, and contribute to its forum.
